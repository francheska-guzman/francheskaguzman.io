Connect Four

How to use Connect Four:

Decide who plays first, and click the "Start" button. The first player will play black tokens, while the second with red tokens. Players will alternate turns after every move.

Because is a 7 x 6 grid (42 available spaces), the maximum moves per player is 21. On your turn, go to the top of the grid, and click over the token corresponding to the desired column.

The game automatically stops after one of the player's won (4 in a row), or if it's a tie. To play again, click 'Reset' and then press 'Start'. Good luck!

LIVE | CODE

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
